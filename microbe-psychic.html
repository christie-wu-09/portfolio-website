MICROBE PSYCHIC

Design Question

The scientific findings on the gut microbiome are absolutely fascinating. How can we illuminate the importance and health/social implications of the gut microbiome and reimagine the future of science through speculative design?

Overview

Microbe Psychic is a speculative design project where I envisioned myself as Microbe Psychic who can see, analyze, and manipulate gut microbiomes. For this project, I wrote the story, designed and animated the graphics.

Microbe Extractor

To optimize Microbe Psychic’s superpower. I invented a Microbe Extractor that allowed me to collect microbiomes from fecal matter on the go.

Microbe Ice-cream

After collecting different types of microbiomes, Microbe Psychic will make them into ice cream toppings to share with those in need.
